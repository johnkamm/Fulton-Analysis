## **3\. MORTGAGE STRUCTURE & PAYMENT HOLIDAYS**

### **3.0 Base Loan Parameters**

The purchase facility will be financed under a commercial mortgage with the following core financial terms:

* **Loan Term:** 5 Years  
* **Amortization:** 10 Years  
* **Interest Rate:** 8.5% Fixed

To preserve working capital during retrofits and crop ramp-up, debt service under this mortgage is structured in three phases:

| Timeline Phase | Period | Terms & Conditions |
| :---- | :---: | :---- |
| **Phase 1: Initial Holiday** *(Item 3.1)* | Month 1 *(or Construction)* | No interest or mortgage payment |
| **Phase 2: Secondary Holiday** *(Item 3.2)* | Months 2–11 *(10 Months)* | Mortgage payment holiday; contractual note interest (8.5%) to accrue. |
| **Phase 3: Full Amortization** | Month 12+ | Standard principal & interest schedule resumes based on 10-year amortization. |

### **Phase Breakdown**

* **3.1 Initial Holiday (Month 1):** Isolates working capital while contractors execute mechanical repairs, complete electrical additions, and bring cooling/desiccant systems online. No debt service overhead is incurred prior to crop setup.  
* **3.2 Secondary Holiday (Months 2–11):** Protects liquidity through initial cultivation and harvest cycles. Pausing cash outflows for debt service allows early operational revenue to accumulate as unencumbered reserves before entering formal amortization.
